MECH

Mechanical engineers work on cars, planes, tools, weapons and machines, while civil engineers work on buildings and infrastructure. Mechanical engineers focus on an object's function, while civil engineers focus on an object's structure and appearance.

CIVIL

The main scope of civil engineering is planning, designing, estimating, supervising, managing and maintenance of structures like building, roads, bridges, dams

ECE

An electronics and communication engineer can work in aviation and avionics, consumer electronics, electricity plant, manufacturing, distribution, communication & telecommunication, computer application, radio & television, analytical equipment manufacturing and offshore industries

EEE

Electrical engineers can also apply for respective and relevant vacancies coming out of State-Electricity Boards, PWD department, Thermal Power Plants, Nuclear Power Stations, Hydro-electric Power Stations, Solar Power plants, Indian Army, Indian Airforce, Indian Navy, and Indian Railways.

CSE

Professionals holding a computer science and engineering degree can work as Computer Engineers, Lecturers, IT Team Leads, Data Scientists, Systems Managers, Software Engineers, ML Researcher, AI Engineer, Software Developer, Systems Analyst, and Systems Engineers across different industries.

BME

The scope for BME is everything around the improvement of human health and healthcare systems. By blending the power of technology with biomedicine, biomedical engineers have created many life-saving medical devices to increase the scope of biomedical engineering in our lives.
